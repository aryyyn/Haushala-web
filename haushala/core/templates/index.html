AI-Powered Mental Health Support

"Haushala," meaning "courage" in Nepali, is more than just an app — it's your mobile companion on the journey to better mental well-being. This mobile application is designed to offer guidance, provide resources, and lend a supportive hand to those navigating mental health challenges.

Powered by AI, Haushala will empower you with personalized mental health insights, strategies, and resources, tailored specifically to your needs. Whether you're at home or on the go, Haushala is here to support you.
